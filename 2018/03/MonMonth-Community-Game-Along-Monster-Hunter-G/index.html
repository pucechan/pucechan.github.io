Anne over at Chic-Pixel organises a whole slew of monthly game alongs (as well as a ton of other awesome nerdy content) and this month is #MonMonth, featuring Monster Hunter(-like) games.

I decided to actually participate this month so I broke out the Wii Classic Controller (Thanks @Acelister!!!) and decided to attempt to play through the Japanese only Wii port of Monster Hunter G. This port of the expanded game has a few changes to the PS2 original, changing the control scheme and adding new monsters amongst other things.

I streamed the first 8 or so quests on my Twitch/Mixer channels a couple of days ago and hope to do my next stream this weekend. I don’t actually know Japanese at all (outside of recognising some Kana) so me figuring stuff out should be quite amusing and I try and involve the chat as I attempt to make progress.

I highly recommend getting involved, check out Anne’s post, and tweet your videos/screenshots and comments with the #MonMonth hashtag!
